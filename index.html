School Classes

We offer a complete primary program and a strong secondary curriculum with specialized tracks for students to grow.

Primary School

Our Primary program spans Primary 1 through Primary 5 with a nurturing learning environment for young learners.

Classes:

  • Primary 1 – Foundation skills, reading, numeracy, and creative learning
  • Primary 2 – Building confidence in literacy and numeracy
  • Primary 3 – Expanding science, social studies, and arts
  • Primary 4 – Stronger skills in mathematics and language arts
  • Primary 5 – Preparatory learning for junior secondary school

Secondary School

Secondary classes range from JSS1 to SS2, offering a broad curriculum and specialized pathways in senior classes.

Tracks:

  • Art Class – Visual arts, literature, music, and creative design.
  • Science Class – Physics, chemistry, biology, and mathematics with practical labs.
  • Commercial Class – Business studies, accounting, economics, and entrepreneurship.
